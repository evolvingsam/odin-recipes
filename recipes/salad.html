Salad

Description

A fresh and healthy salad with a mix of greens, vegetables, and a light dressing.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens.
  2. Slice the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
