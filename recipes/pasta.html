Pasta

Description

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add marinara sauce and Italian seasoning to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
