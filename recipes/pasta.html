Pasta

Description

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes, crushed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.
